For the fig-swirl: melt butter over medium heat in a saucepen. Add brown sugar and stir dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completly.


Ice cream: In small pot over medium heat, combine milk and granulated sugar until sugar is completly dissolved and the milk us just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled


Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instruction, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 ,minutes before adding mascarpon, fig mixture, and the nuts. Contuniue to process fro +/- 19 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperwaew. Freez for at least two hours before serving.

  • 11/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1lb black brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 11/2 cups heavy cream
  • 4/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecane, roughly chopped
